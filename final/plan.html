Final Project Site Plan

Site Name

Paraguay Missionary Portal

Site Purpose

This site provides missionaries who previously served, or are currently serving, in Paraguay and their families with access to information, services, and products from Paraguay.

Users will be able to:

  • Find a courier address to send packages to and register the package information.
  • Purchase local and imported products to be shipped to the USA or to a local Mission Office.
  • Search through a list of recipes of Paraguayan food.

Scenarios

  • How can I send a package in a reliable way to my missionary child in Paraguay?
  • How can I purchase local and imported products for my missionary child?
  • Where can I purchase my favorite Paraguayan products from the mission and have them shipped back to the United States?
  • Where do I find recipes for my favorite dishes that I ate when I was serving a mission in Paraguay?

Typography

The "Inter" font will be used for all text. Heading and Button text will be bolded with a font weight of 700. All other text will have a font weight of 400.
